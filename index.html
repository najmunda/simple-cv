SimpleCV?

SimpleCV is a simple web app for making a simple cv. SimpleCV use local storage to save your CV and CV "part", make your CV-making experience easier. SimpleCV is developed as media for my Web Development learning, my portofolio on Web Developing & for my job-seeking sake (yes I'll used it myself).

SimpleCV Feature

  • Simple

    • Using a very simple CV so you dont waste your time on useless factor.

  • Several CV

    • SimpleCV will save last CV and you can modifiy your CV based on role you aim.

  • Drag & Drop

    • Copy-Paste is simple, but drag & drop is much simpler.

  • One-click export

    • Export your CV in just one click.

  • Access on everywhere

    • Simpler interface faciliate you to make your CV on various screen size.

  • Client-side App

    • Make CV without internet, also your information is lived only on your device.

How to use

  1. Fill your name and brief explaination

    2. Fill it on the beginning fields.

  2. Make some CV section

    3. Make section like "Educations" or "Experiences".

  3. Fill it with some item

    4. Fill your section with "item". An item will have 3 details: title, time-detail, and description.

  4. Drag & Drop

    5. Drag & drop section/item you've made and arrange it with hope your CV will look best.
